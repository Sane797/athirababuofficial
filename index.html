About Me

Myself Athira Babu, a passionate and enthusiastic Beginner Trading Mentor, dedicated to empowering new traders to reach their financial goals with confidence and clarity. With a solid foundation in education and a deep interest in trading, I specialize in creating personalized trading plans, offering simplified market analysis, and guiding learners through practical trading strategies.

I bring a learner-friendly approach to mentoring, shaped by my background in elementary education (D.El.Ed) and technical training from KGTE. My strengths lie in clear communication, time management, and a deep commitment to helping others grow through consistent support and personalized guidance.
